Tooltip

A tooltip is a short text label that provides supplementary information about an element. It appears near its trigger and stays hidden until activated.

Tooltips can be used to clarify the purpose of icon buttons, abbreviations, or other interface elements that benefit from a brief explanation. They are not suited for essential information, as they can be difficult to discover for some users.

Accessibility Features

= Required = Recommended
  • role="tooltip" on the tooltip element identifies it to screen readers
  • aria-expanded on the trigger button communicates the expanded or collapsed state to screen readers
  • Activating the tooltip through a dedicated toggle button is more reliable than hover- or focus-based activation, which can behave inconsistently on touchscreens
  • The tooltip can be dismissed by pressing the button again, clicking outside, or pressing Esc without moving focus
