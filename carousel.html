Carousel

A carousel cycles through several content panels in the same area of the page. There are two variants: a slide carousel that displays one item at a time with navigation buttons, and a card carousel that displays several smaller items at once and scrolls as the user moves focus between them.

Card Carousel

Displays several cards side by side. The carousel scrolls automatically as the user moves focus between cards.

Slide Carousel

Displays one slide at a time. Users navigate between slides with previous and next buttons or indicator dots.
